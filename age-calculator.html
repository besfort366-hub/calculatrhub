How to use the age calculator

Our free age calculator tells you exactly how old you are — down to the years, months, days, hours and even minutes. Simply enter your date of birth and optionally a target date to calculate your age at any point in time. The calculator also shows your next birthday countdown, total days lived, total weeks, and fun facts like the day of the week you were born and your zodiac sign.

How is it calculated?

Age is calculated by finding the exact difference between your date of birth and the target date. The calculator accounts for leap years and varying month lengths to give you a precise result in years, months and days. It also converts this into total days, weeks, hours and minutes lived.

Frequently asked questions

How do I calculate my exact age?

To calculate your exact age, subtract your date of birth from today's date accounting for leap years. Our calculator does this automatically and gives you the result in years, months and days simultaneously.

What day of the week was I born?

Enter your date of birth in our age calculator and it will instantly tell you what day of the week you were born — whether that's Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday or any other day.

How many days old am I?

The number of days you've lived is calculated by multiplying your age in years by 365, adding days for leap years, plus any remaining months and days. For a 30-year-old, that's roughly 10,950+ days.

What is my zodiac sign?

Your zodiac sign is determined by your birth date. Aries: Mar 21–Apr 19, Taurus: Apr 20–May 20, Gemini: May 21–Jun 20, Cancer: Jun 21–Jul 22, Leo: Jul 23–Aug 22, Virgo: Aug 23–Sep 22, and so on.

Can I calculate my age on a future date?

Yes — our date calculator lets you set any target date, past or future, to calculate what your age will be on that date. Useful for planning milestones like retirement age or a big birthday.

How many hours have I been alive?

Multiply your age in days by 24 to get hours lived. A 25-year-old has lived approximately 219,000 hours. Our calculator shows this automatically when you enter your date of birth.
